Brownie

Description

A chocolate brownie is a rich, square or rectangular baked dessert bar, often with a moist and fudgy texture, and a deep chocolate flavor.

Ingredients

  • Dark Chocolate - 200g chopped
  • Butter - 1/2 cup or 115g
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Eggs - 2 large
  • Vanilla Extract - 1 tablespoon
  • All purpose flour(maida) - 1/2 cup
  • Cocoa Powder - 2 tablespoon
  • Salt - a pinch
  • Choclate chips - 1/2 cups

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F) and grease or line a square baking pan.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together in a bowl over simmering water or microwave.
  3. Whisk in sugar until smooth and slightly cooled.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Stir in vanilla extract.
  6. Sift in flour, cocoa powder, and salt; fold gently into the batter.
  7. Mix in extra chocolate chips.
  8. Pour batter into the prepared pan and spread evenly
  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out with moist crumbs.
  10. Cool completely before cutting into squares and serving.
