Pav Bahji

Description

Pav Bhaji is a delicious street food from Mumbai. It is a blend of spicy mashed vegetables served with butter toasted buns.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes - 3-4 medium , boiled and mashed
  • Cauliflower - 1 cup , finely chopped
  • Green Peas - 1/2 cup
  • Carrot - 1-2 medium , chopped
  • Onions - 2 medium , finely chopped
  • Capsicum - 1 finely chopped
  • Tomatoes - 34 large , pured
  • Ginger-garlic Paste - 1 tablespoom
  • Turmeric Powder - 1/2 tanlespoon
  • Red chilli powder - 1 tanlespoon
  • PavBhaji Masala - 2-3 tanlespoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Green Chillies - 1-2 finely chopped
  • Butter - 2-3 tablespoon
  • Water - as needed
  • Cilantro - for garnish
  • Lemon - for serving
  • Pav buns - 4 for single serving

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes, cauliflower, peas, and carrots until soft.
  2. Lightly mash the boiled vegetables and set aside
  3. Finely chop onions, tomatoes, capsicum, and green chilies.
  4. Heat butter in a pan and sauté chopped onions until golden.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft and mushy.
  7. Add chopped capsicum and cook for 2-3 minutes
  8. Mix in pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt.
  9. Add mashed vegetables and mix well with the masala.
  10. Mash everything together to combine flavors.
  11. Add water as needed and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  12. Add a knob of butter and garnish with chopped coriander.
  13. Heat butter on a tawa and sprinkle a little pav bhaji masala.
  14. Slit pav buns and toast them on both sides until golden.
  15. Serve bhaji hot with buttered pav, lemon wedges, and chopped onions.
