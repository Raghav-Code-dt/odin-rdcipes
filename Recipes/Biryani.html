Biryani

Description

Veg Biryani is a flavourful rice dish made with basmati rice, mixed vegetables, whole spices, freshly ginger, garlic, coriander and mint leaves.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice - 2 cups(soaked for 30 mins)
  • Whole Species - 1 bay leaf, 4 cloves, 2 cardamoms, 1 cinnamon stick
  • Water - for boiling
  • Mixed vegetables - 2 cups (carrot, beans, peas, potato, cauliflower)
  • Onion - 2 large, thinly sliced
  • Tomato - 1 medium, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Green chillies - 2 slit
  • Yogurt - 1/2 cup
  • Red chilli Powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Turmeric Powder - 1/2 tablespoon
  • Garam Masala - 1/2 tablespoon
  • Biryani Masala - 1/2 tablespoon
  • Ghee or oil - 3-4 tablespoon
  • Saffron - a pinch soaked in 2 tablespoon warm milk
  • Fried Onions - for garnish
  • Fresh mint and coriander - for garnish
  • Salt - to taste

Steps

  1. Soak basmati rice and parboil it with whole spices and salt, then drain.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pan and sauté sliced onions until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; sauté for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook till soft.
  5. Mix in chopped vegetables and sauté for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Add yogurt, turmeric, red chili, biryani masala, and garam masala; mix well.
  7. Add mint, coriander, and salt; cook until veggies are nearly done.
  8. In a heavy-bottomed pot, layer half the rice over the cooked veggies.
  9. Add a layer of fried onions, mint, coriander, and saffron milk.
  10. Repeat rice layer and toppings if needed; drizzle ghee on top.
  11. Cover tightly with lid or dough and cook on low flame for 20-25 minutes (dum).
  12. Let it rest for 10 minutes, then gently fluff and serve hot.
